Contact CJF Support

Report scams, fraud, or contact us for assistance. All reports are reviewed promptly.

⚠️ Remember: CJF does not handle payments, refunds, or disputes. Contact Hosts directly for rental issues.

Reporting Scams

If you encounter a scam or fraudulent listing:

  • Select "Report Scam / Fraud" in the issue type above
  • Include the Host's name, listing URL, and description of the scam
  • Provide screenshots or evidence if available
  • We will investigate all reports within 24-48 hours

Note: Hosts with 7 verified scam reports are permanently banned from CJF.
