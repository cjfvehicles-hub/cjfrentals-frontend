Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 7, 2025

1. Information We Collect

CJF collects minimal information necessary to operate the platform:

  • Basic contact information: Name, email, phone number
  • Host verification details: Address, license information (optional), owner type
  • Vehicle information: Photos, descriptions, pricing, location
  • Account data: Login credentials, profile settings

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Operate and maintain the CJF platform
  • Display Host listings to potential Renters
  • Enable communication between Hosts and Renters
  • Verify Host identity (basic checks only)
  • Send platform updates and notifications
  • Improve platform safety and functionality

3. What We Do NOT Do

CJF does NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties or advertisers
  • Share your data with marketing companies
  • Track your browsing across other websites
  • Use invasive analytics or tracking cookies
  • Rent or lease your personal information

4. Data Sharing

Your data is only shared in the following cases:

  • Public listings: Vehicle information and Host contact details are visible to potential Renters
  • Law enforcement: CJF may disclose information if legally required or in cases of fraud, stolen vehicles, identity theft, or safety threats
  • Service providers: Minimal data may be shared with hosting/email providers necessary for platform operation

We will never sell your data for profit.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Secure database storage
  • Password encryption
  • Limited employee access
  • Regular security reviews

However: No online platform is 100% secure. Users are responsible for protecting their own accounts and login credentials.

6. Cookies & Tracking

CJF uses minimal cookies for:

  • User authentication (login sessions)
  • Basic functionality (remembering preferences)
  • Safety popup acknowledgment

We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt out of platform communications (excluding essential notifications)

To exercise these rights, contact us at CJF Support.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • While your account is active
  • For 30 days after account deletion (for fraud prevention)
  • Longer if required by law or ongoing investigations

9. Children's Privacy

CJF is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover underage accounts, they will be deleted immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

CJF may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
